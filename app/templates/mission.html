Mission:

Weconnec intends to resolve the need for a central
platform that allows, primarily students to share resources
among themselves. Often times barriers impede how
effective study resources are shared among students. One
such barrier is association. Students may not know the
right persons to be affiliated with in order to get
helpful resources. In addition, the project also
seeks to enhance how efficient students can
manipulate study resources.
